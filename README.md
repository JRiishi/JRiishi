## About
I work at the intersection of **Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision**, with a strong focus on understanding the **mathematics and theory behind models**—not just using them.

My goal is simple: build systems that are not only functional, but technically sound and scalable. Most of my work revolves around turning theoretical concepts into real, working implementations.
